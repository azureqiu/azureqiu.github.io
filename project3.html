This project is the bracelet I come up with during the class. I just want to finish the concept. Once user wear the bracelet entering a zoo, it will detect different animals with a digital thermo-sensor. The bracelet will be divided into 8 panels which towards 8 directions which will guide you with vibration and alteration of color. Different color which stands for different animals will show up on each penal of the bracelet and the bracelet would vibrate once the animal gets close.
