Stranger Project
Mirror with LED light show up at the back.

After research and observation, we discover that when people pass a reflective curtain wall and mirror, they used to take a look of themselves to check if they look nice and fully dressed. Therefore, we decide to pop out some encourage quotes on the mirror to praise them and make their day.

Process

Material

Reasearch of "How to say 'you look good'"


Working....
Testing....



First iteration

We just use window film without any support at the back. It doesn't work really well since it is unable to reflect figure. Also, the wind in in the subway is really strong.The surface can't stay flat.

The problem we found:The lower leds texts are too blurry to see
The distance sensor need to be triggered more easily

Second iteration

Improve: Change the mirror material’s cover Use black tape to cover the white strip of the leds Extend the trigger distance of the sensor

The problem we found:In subway we are restricted to put it on the side. People doesn’t look into the mirror directly

Therefore, we change display space to a more open space in next iteration.

Third iteration

It attracks more people in union square.


Conclusion

Text showcase can be more clear Sentence should look more directly and shows continuity Investigate the mindset of the audience Should get a larger mirror

If we get chance, we will definitely push this project forward. We will research more and explore more different materials.
